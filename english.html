About

Yoshitaka Amano

Considered as one of the most influential japanese painter of its time, Yoshitaka Amano is well known for his amazing work on the Final Fantasy saga and the manga Vampire Hunter D. Infamous creator with fairy fingers, that man starts his pro-career at 15 inside the prestigigious Tatsunoko Animation company. Specialized in character design, his talent is well known all around Japan, but also in the USA and Europe.

Gallery

Odin

Odin is a recurring summon in Final Fantasy. He made his debut in Final Fantasy III and several of his appearances allude to his original encounter.

Ifrit

Ifrit (イフリート, Ifurīto), also called Jinn and Iflyte, is a recurring Fire-elemental summon in the series. He is one of the most frequently appearing summons and is often a rival to the ice summon Shiva.

Ramuh

Ramuh (ラムウ, Ramū), also known as Indra, is a recurring character and summoned creature in the Final Fantasy series. He is affiliated with thunder and lightning, and appears as an old man carrying a staff. His signature attack is Judgment Bolt.
